Cats ? In Space ?

Kittens should absolutely not be sent into space. And this is for very serious scientific reasons.

First, a cat in a rocket is still a cat. And a cat sees a red button… and presses it. Generally, red buttons are a bad idea.

Secondly, in zero gravity, a cat doesn't land on its feet… it floats. The result: a small ball of fur in orbit, annoyed, plotting its revenge.

Thirdly (and most seriously), cats might discover they are already the masters of the universe. Feline supremacy would then begin, and the world would fall at the cats' paws...

Conclusion: For the safety of humanity, the galaxy, and the universe, let's leave cats alone on Earth.
